From booking care to intelligent follow-up, every handoff stays visible.

The workflow transforms healthcare coordination into a calm connected experience where booking, clinical review, diagnostics, reports, prescriptions, and AI support move as one operating system.

The experience was designed to reduce cognitive overload across appointments, diagnostics, prescriptions, notifications, and multi-role workflows.

Role Ecosystem

One healthcare network, four focused operating views.

PT

Patient

Books care, views reports, and follows prescriptions.

DR

Doctor

Reviews history, schedules consults, and creates care plans.

LB

Lab

Tracks diagnostics, uploads reports, and closes test queues.

AD

Admin

Manages operations, users, payments, and workflow health.
